TRADING

Stock market trading is the activity of buying and selling shares of companies that are listed on a
stock exchange. A stock exchange is a place where buyers and sellers meet to trade shares at an
agreed price.The price of a share depends on the demand and supply of the share in the market,
as well as the performance and prospects of the company.
